About SurahFlow

Assalamu Alaikum,

I built SurahFlow because I wanted a simple way to memorize and review the Quran consistently myself. As I used it, I realized it could help others on the same journey, so I decided to make it available for everyone.

My goal is to keep this tool free, simple, and beneficial for anyone seeking to strengthen their connection with the Quran.

If SurahFlow has benefited you, please consider supporting the project. Your support helps keep SurahFlow free, ad-free, and continuously improving.

You can choose any support amount on the next page. Every contribution, no matter the size, is sincerely appreciated.

May Allah accept our efforts and make the Quran a source of guidance and blessing in our lives.

— Adam Yadranji
